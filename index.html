MLL Mobility Private Limited

Meru is a pioneering brand in the shared mobility space in India, that is a part of MLL Mobility Private Limited. Belonging to the Mahindra Group lineage, MLL Mobility is a leading mobility solutions provider with over a decade of experience in offering integrated, customized, scalable, innovative and technology-enabled solutions to its customers across diverse industries. Leveraging on the strong foundation provided by MLL's capabilities and resources, Meru now aims to take its service offerings to newer heights, delivering on its promise of customer excellence, safety and sustainability.
